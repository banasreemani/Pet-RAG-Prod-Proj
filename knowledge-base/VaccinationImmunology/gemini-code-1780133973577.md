### Abstract
Canine core vaccinations protect against high-mortality viral pathogens: Canine Distemper Virus (CDV), Canine Parvovirus type 2 (CPV-2), Canine Adenovirus (CAV-1/CAV-2), and Rabies.

### The Maternal Antibody Conundrum
Neonates receive transient immunity via colostrum (maternally derived antibodies, or MDA). While MDA protects puppies early on, it also neutralizes vaccine antigens, preventing the puppy's immune system from building its own long-term response.
