### Abstract
Feline core vaccines address Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis (FHV-1), Feline Calicivirus (FCV), Feline Panleukopenia (FPV), and Rabies. This article outlines administration rules designed to lower the risk of feline injection-site sarcomas.

### Vaccine Anatomy & Pathogenesis of FISS
Feline Injection-Site Sarcomas (FISS) are aggressive, invasive malignant tumors that can form at injection sites, often linked to local inflammation from adjuvanted vaccines.
